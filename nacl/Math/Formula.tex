\subsubsection{Burnside's Lemma}
Let $X$ be a set and $G$ be a group that acts on $X$.
For $g \in G$, denote by $X^g$ the elements fixed by $g$:
\[
X^g = \{ x \in X \mid gx \in X \}
\]
Then
\[
|X/G| = \frac{1}{|G|} \sum_{g \in G} |X^g|.
\]
